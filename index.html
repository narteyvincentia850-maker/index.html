About BrainCoins

BrainCoins is a free play-to-earn trivia game where you answer questions to earn virtual coins. Test your knowledge across Science, History, Pop Culture, Geography, Sports, Technology and more!

How It Works

Pick a category and difficulty, answer 5 AI-generated questions, and earn coins for every correct answer. Use your coins in the shop to unlock new categories, themes and badges.

Who Made This?

BrainCoins was created by Vincentia Nartey, an entrepreneur from Ghana building fun and educational tools for everyone.

Our Mission

We believe learning should be fun and rewarding. BrainCoins makes trivia exciting by giving players something to earn and work towards.
